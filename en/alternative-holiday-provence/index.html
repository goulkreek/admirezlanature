Unusual accommodation in Provence

When was your last romantic vacation?

Admirez La Nature is the essential destination to get away from it all and get away from the crowds, holiday clubs and noise.

Stay differently and play the atypical card with our unusual guest room.

Isolated in the woods, our atypical rooms have a panoramic view that provides escapism and freedom, so you can admire all the magical beauty and calm that Mother Nature offers you.

Guest rooms reserved for adults (from 15 years old) each adult has a vital need for rest and silence to regain all their energy.

Our goal: Disconnect you from everyday life by offering you a moment of relaxation just for you!

TV. and wifi will be voluntarily absent on the site.
